# A Kiss

## 📖 Story

Carlos buys a new car. It's a very [[Expensive|expensive]] car. It's a [[Huge|huge]], blue, fast car. While driving down the street, Carlos sees a girl on a bicycle. She has long blond hair and is beautiful.

He [[Yell|yells]] to her, "What's up?"

She ignores him.

He [[Yell|yells]], "How's it going?"

She keeps going and ignores him.

He [[Yell|yells]], "Hey, why won't you talk to me? I want to go to dinner with you. I'll take you to an [[Expensive|expensive]] restaurant."

The girl turns, gets off the bike, and looks at him. She says, "I don't want to go to dinner. But if you give me your car, I will give you a surprise."

Carlos says, "OK!" He jumps out of the car. He gives her the keys and says, "Here are the keys."

The beautiful blond takes the keys and then kisses Carlos on the cheek. Then she jumps into the car and drives away.

Carlos stands on the sidewalk. Now he has no car and no girl. He says, "That's it, just a kiss on the cheek?"

He gets on her bike and rides home.

## 📚 Vocabulary

### Key Words & Phrases

| Word/Phrase | Meaning | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| **huge** /hjuːdʒ/ | Rất lớn, khổng lồ | It's a huge, blue, fast car. |
| **yell** /jel/ | Hét, la to | He yells to her, "What's up?" |
| **ignore** /ɪɡˈnɔːr/ | Lờ đi, phát ngờ | She ignores him. |
| **keep going** | Tiếp tục đi | She keeps going and ignores him. |

| **jump out of** | Nhảy ra khỏi | He jumps out of the car. |
| **on the cheek** | Trên má | She kisses Carlos on the cheek. |
| **drive away** | Lái xe đi mất | She drives away. |
| **sidewalk** /ˈsaɪdwɔːk/ | Vỉa hè | Carlos stands on the sidewalk. |
| **That's it?** | Chỉ thế thôi sao? | That's it, just a kiss on the cheek? |

### Useful Expressions

- **What's up?** - Có chuyện gì? / Sao rồi?
- **How's it going?** - Mọi thứ thế nào?
- **Why won't you...?** - Tại sao bạn không...?
- **I want to go to dinner with you** - Tôi muốn đi ăn tối với bạn
- **I'll take you to...** - Tôi sẽ đưa bạn đến...
- **Here are the keys** - Đây là chìa khóa

## 📝 Grammar Points

### 1. Present Simple for Actions
```
Carlos buys a new car.
She has long blond hair.
He yells to her.
```

### 2. Present Continuous for Ongoing Actions
```
While driving down the street...
She keeps going and ignores him.
```

### 3. Future with "will"
```
I will give you a surprise.
I'll take you to an expensive restaurant.
```

### 4. Conditional Sentences
```
If you give me your car, I will give you a surprise.
```

## 🎯 Key Takeaways

### Vocabulary Focus
- **Vehicles**: car, bicycle/bike
- **Actions**: yell, ignore, jump, drive, ride
- **Descriptions**: huge, expensive, beautiful, blond

### Grammar Focus
- Simple present tense for narrative
- Conditional sentences (if...will...)
- Direct speech

### Cultural Notes
- In English-speaking countries, yelling at strangers on the street is generally considered rude
- "What's up?" and "How's it going?" are informal greetings
- This story teaches a lesson about impulsive decisions
