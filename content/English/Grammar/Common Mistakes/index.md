# Common Mistakes

## ❌ Frequent Errors for Vietnamese Speakers

### Articles (a, an, the)
- ❌ "We need implement caching"
- ✅ "We need **to** implement **a** caching **solution**"

- ❌ "The performance is issue"
- ✅ "**The** performance is **an** issue"

### Prepositions
- ❌ "Depend of the configuration"
- ✅ "Depend **on** the configuration"

- ❌ "Listen at port 8080"
- ✅ "Listen **on** port 8080"

- ❌ "Consists from multiple services"
- ✅ "Consists **of** multiple services"

### Subject-Verb Agreement
- ❌ "The data **are** corrupted" (data as singular)
- ✅ "The data **is** corrupted" (in computing context)

- ❌ "Each service **have** their own database"
- ✅ "Each service **has** **its** own database"

### Singular vs Plural
- ❌ "We have many **informations**"
- ✅ "We have much **information**" (uncountable)

- ❌ "These **softwares**"
- ✅ "These **software packages**" or "This **software**"

## 🔤 Word Choice Errors

### Affect vs Effect
- ❌ "This change will **effect** performance"
- ✅ "This change will **affect** performance" (verb)
- ✅ "The **effect** of this change is positive" (noun)

### Its vs It's
- ❌ "The system has **it's** own cache"
- ✅ "The system has **its** own cache" (possessive)
- ✅ "**It's** a distributed system" (It is)

### Then vs Than
- ❌ "This algorithm is faster **then** that one"
- ✅ "This algorithm is faster **than** that one"
- ✅ "First authenticate, **then** authorize"

### Less vs Fewer
- ❌ "**Less** requests"
- ✅ "**Fewer** requests" (countable)
- ✅ "**Less** latency" (uncountable)

## ⚙️ Technical Writing Mistakes

### Redundancy
- ❌ "Completely eliminate"
- ✅ "Eliminate"

- ❌ "Advance planning"
- ✅ "Planning"

### Wordiness
- ❌ "Due to the fact that"
- ✅ "Because"

- ❌ "In order to"
- ✅ "To"

- ❌ "At this point in time"
- ✅ "Now"

### Ambiguity
- ❌ "This is good"
- ✅ "This approach improves performance by 30%"

- ❌ "It doesn't work"
- ✅ "The authentication service returns a 500 error"
